Backtest Scoring Methodology

Gap Size Performance (25%)

Historical data shows 2-10% gaps have the best success rates (42-52%). Extreme gaps (>10%) tend to fade with only 28% continuation rate.

Pre-Earnings Trend (30%)

Stocks with stable upward trends (0-20%) before earnings show highest continuation probability. Post-decline bounces carry higher risk.

Volume Trend (20%)

Healthy volume increase (100-120%) indicates growing institutional interest. Abnormal spikes (>120%) often signal instability.

MA200 Position (15%)

Long-term trend indicator. Stocks trading above 200MA show stronger momentum sustainability.

MA50 Position (10%)

Medium-term trend confirmation. Above MA50 indicates healthy short-term momentum.
