Backtest Scoring Methodology

1. Gap Size Performance (25%)

Evaluates the initial post-earnings gap size. Moderate gaps (0-5%) historically show better continuation rates than extreme gaps (>10%).

2. Pre-Earnings Trend (30%)

Analyzes the 20-day price trend before earnings. Stocks with stable-to-strong uptrends (0-20%) tend to have higher success rates.

3. Volume Trend (20%)

Compares recent 20-day average volume to 60-day historical average. Rising volume (100-120%) indicates healthy institutional interest.

4. MA200 Position (15%)

Stock's position relative to 200-day moving average. Stocks above MA200 (>105%) show stronger long-term bullish momentum.

5. MA50 Position (10%)

Stock's position relative to 50-day moving average. Confirms medium-term trend alignment with earnings momentum.
