Scoring Methodology

Gap Size Performance 25%

Measures the initial gap-up magnitude. Larger gaps (5%+) typically indicate stronger institutional interest and higher success rates for continuation.

Pre-Earnings Trend 30%

Evaluates the 20-day price trend before earnings. Stocks in established uptrends with positive momentum score higher for post-earnings continuation.

Volume Trend Analysis 20%

Compares current volume to average. Higher relative volume (2x+) indicates institutional participation and validates the price move.

MA200 Position 15%

Price position relative to 200-day moving average. Trading above MA200 indicates long-term bullish trend and institutional support.

MA50 Position 10%

Price position relative to 50-day moving average. Measures intermediate-term momentum and trend strength.
