Scoring Methodology

Gap Size (25% weight)

2-5% optimal gap achieves highest historical success rates. Gaps >10% face increased reversal risk.

Pre-Earnings Trend (30% weight)

Stocks with >20% pre-earnings momentum show strongest follow-through. Negative trends indicate recovery plays.

Volume Trend (20% weight)

110-120% volume ratio signals healthy accumulation. Abnormal volume >120% may indicate instability.

MA200 Position (15% weight)

Price well above 200-day MA indicates strong long-term trend. Below MA suggests recovery scenario.

MA50 Position (10% weight)

Medium-term trend confirmation. Stocks above both MAs show strongest momentum patterns.
