Backtest Scoring Methodology

Gap Size (25% weight)

Evaluates the initial gap-up percentage. Moderate gaps (2-5%) historically show highest continuation rates.

Pre-Earnings Trend (30% weight)

Analyzes price action 20 days before earnings. Strong uptrends (>20%) typically see continued momentum.

Volume Trend (20% weight)

Compares recent 20-day volume vs 60-day average. Increasing volume (100-120%) indicates institutional interest.

MA200 Position (15% weight)

Distance from 200-day moving average. Stocks well above (>110%) show strong long-term trends.

MA50 Position (10% weight)

Distance from 50-day moving average. Above MA50 indicates medium-term bullish momentum.
