Backtest Scoring Methodology

1. Gap Size Performance (25%)

Analyzes the optimal gap size range based on historical success rates. 2-5% gaps show highest follow-through rates at 52%.

2. Pre-Earnings Trend (30%)

Evaluates price momentum leading into earnings. Stocks with 10-20%+ uptrend show strongest continuation patterns.

3. Volume Trend (20%)

Compares recent 20-day average volume to 60-day average. Moderate increases (100-120%) indicate healthy interest.

4. MA200 Position (15%)

Measures long-term trend alignment. Stocks trading 10%+ above 200-day MA indicate strong bullish momentum.

5. MA50 Position (10%)

Assesses medium-term trend strength. Position above 50-day MA confirms near-term upward momentum.
