Company Overview

About Eastman Chemical Company

Founded: 1920 | Headquarters: Kingsport, Tennessee | CEO: Mark J. Costa

Eastman Chemical Company operates as a specialty materials company in the United States and internationally. The company operates through four segments:

  • Additives & Functional Products: Hydrocarbon and rosin resins, specialty polymers, heat transfer fluids, and performance resins for transportation, agriculture, and construction markets
  • Advanced Materials: Copolyesters, cellulosic biopolymers, PVB sheets, and protective films for transportation, electronics, and medical applications
  • Chemical Intermediates: Methylamines, olefin derivatives, ethylene, and plasticizers for industrial chemicals and construction
  • Fibers: Cellulose acetate products, yarns, and specialty papers for filtration media and industrial applications

The company serves diverse end markets including transportation, healthcare, building & construction, electronics, and consumer goods. Eastman invests approximately 4% of sales in R&D and has strategically focused on high-margin specialty products, which now represent 60% of revenue.
