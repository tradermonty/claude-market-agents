Backtest Scoring Methodology

Gap Size Performance (25%)

Measures the gap-up percentage after earnings. Optimal range is 2-10%. Larger gaps (10%+) have lower success rates due to overextension.

Pre-Earnings Trend (30%)

20-day price trend before earnings. Strong upward momentum (10-20%+) indicates institutional accumulation and higher continuation probability.

Volume Trend Analysis (20%)

Recent 20-day vs 60-day average volume ratio. 100-120% indicates healthy interest accumulation. Abnormal volume (120%+) suggests instability.

MA200 Analysis (15%)

Position relative to 200-day moving average. Stocks above MA200 have stronger long-term momentum and institutional support.

MA50 Analysis (10%)

Position relative to 50-day moving average. Indicates medium-term trend strength and recent price momentum.
