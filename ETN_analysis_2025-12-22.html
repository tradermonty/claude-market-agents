Company Overview

Sector: Industrials | Industry: Industrial - Machinery
CEO: Paulo Ruiz Sternadt | Founded: 1911

Eaton Corporation plc operates as a global power management company with operations across electrical, aerospace, vehicle, and eMobility segments. The company provides electrical components, power distribution systems, aerospace pumps and motors, transmission systems, and electric vehicle components to commercial and industrial customers worldwide. Headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, Eaton serves customers in over 175 countries with innovative solutions for energy-efficient power management.
