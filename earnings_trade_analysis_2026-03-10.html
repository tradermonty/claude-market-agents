Scoring Methodology

Gap Size (25%)

Optimal gap range 2-10%. Very large gaps (>10%) often unsustainable.

Pre-Earnings Trend (30%)

20-day price trend before earnings. Strong uptrends (10-20%+) indicate momentum.

Volume Trend (20%)

Recent 20-day vs 60-day average. 100-120% optimal. >120% signals instability.

MA200 Position (15%)

Position relative to 200-day MA. Above MA indicates long-term bullish trend.

MA50 Position (10%)

Position relative to 50-day MA. Above MA indicates medium-term momentum.
