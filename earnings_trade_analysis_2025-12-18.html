Backtest Scoring Methodology

This analysis uses a 5-factor backtest scoring system based on historical earnings gap-up performance data. Each factor is weighted according to its predictive power:

  • Gap Size (25% weight): Optimal gaps of 0-5% have highest success rates (42-52%); large gaps (10%+) have only 28% success
  • Pre-Earnings Trend (30% weight): Stocks with positive pre-earnings momentum tend to continue post-earnings
  • Volume Analysis (20% weight): Volume 100-120% of average is ideal; extreme volume (>120%) often indicates reversal risk
  • MA200 Position (15% weight): Stocks above 200-day MA show stronger follow-through
  • MA50 Position (10% weight): Near-term trend confirmation above 50-day MA

Grade Interpretation:
A-Grade (85+ pts): 70%+ historical success rate | B-Grade (70-84 pts): 55-69% success | C-Grade (55-69 pts): 40-54% success | D-Grade (<55 pts): <40% success
