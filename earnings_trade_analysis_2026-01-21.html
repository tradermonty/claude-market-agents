Backtest Scoring Methodology

Gap Size Performance 25%

Evaluates EPS and revenue surprise magnitude. Higher surprises (>15% EPS, >5% Rev) score higher. Based on historical correlation between surprise size and post-earnings momentum continuation.

Pre-Earnings Trend 30%

Analyzes price action in the 4 weeks before earnings. Strong uptrend (+10%+) with controlled pullbacks scores highest. Assesses institutional positioning before the announcement.

Volume Trend Analysis 20%

Compares recent volume to 20-day average. Rising volume on up days with decreasing volume on down days indicates accumulation. High relative volume on earnings day is a positive signal.

MA200 Analysis 15%

Stocks trading above 200-day MA indicate long-term bullish trend. Distance from MA200 (5-20% optimal) and recent crossover direction are considered.

MA50 Analysis 10%

50-day MA provides intermediate trend context. Price above MA50 with MA50 above MA200 (golden cross) scores highest. Used to confirm trend alignment.
