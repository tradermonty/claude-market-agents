Key Risk Factors

AI Capex Digestion Risk

Hyperscaler AI capex could slow in 2027 if ROI materializes slower than expected. Affects: NVDA, AVGO, ANET, TSM, MU, AMD, LRCX, MTSI, MPWR (portfolio ~70%).

Technology Sector Concentration

11/19 stocks are in Technology. A sector rotation or multiple compression event would impact the entire portfolio. Mitigated partially by GE, FIX, POWL, KTOS, RKLB, MNST diversification.

Geopolitical / Trade War

China export restrictions (NVDA, AMD), Taiwan risk (TSM), tariffs (NXT), and China regulatory (BABA) represent significant geopolitical exposure.

Valuation Risk

Several stocks trade at premium multiples: RKLB (48x revenue), KTOS (high P/E), AVGO, AMD. A rate rise or risk-off event could compress multiples significantly.

Memory Cycle Peak

MU's supercycle could peak by late 2027 if all 3 major players (Samsung, SK Hynix, Micron) overbuild HBM/DRAM capacity simultaneously.

Defense Budget Uncertainty

KTOS and RKLB are sensitive to defense spending decisions. Proposed $1.5T budget for 2027 may face Congressional pushback or re-prioritization.
