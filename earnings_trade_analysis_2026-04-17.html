Backtest Scoring Methodology

Gap Size Performance (25%)

Analyzes the post-earnings gap percentage. Moderate gaps (2-10%) historically show better follow-through than extreme gaps (>10%).

Pre-Earnings Trend (30%)

Evaluates 20-day price momentum before earnings. Strong uptrends (>10%) into earnings indicate institutional accumulation.

Volume Trend Analysis (20%)

Compares recent 20-day volume to 60-day average. Increasing volume (100-120%) signals rising institutional interest.

MA200 Analysis (15%)

Price position relative to 200-day moving average. Stocks >10% above MA200 show strong long-term bullish trends.

MA50 Analysis (10%)

Price position relative to 50-day moving average. Indicates medium-term trend strength and institutional support.
