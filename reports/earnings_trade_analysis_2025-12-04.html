Gap Size Performance (25% weight)

Based on historical analysis of post-earnings gap success rates. Moderate gaps (0-10%) historically outperform extreme gaps (10%+), which often lead to profit-taking.

Pre-Earnings Trend (30% weight)

Relative volume between 100-120% indicates healthy institutional participation. Extreme volume (>120%) often signals exhaustion.

Stocks trading above 200-day MA in uptrends have higher success rates. Position >110% above indicates strong trend.
