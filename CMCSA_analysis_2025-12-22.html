Business Description:

Comcast Corporation operates as a media and technology company worldwide through five segments: Cable Communications, Media, Studios, Theme Parks, and Sky. The company offers broadband, video, voice, wireless services under the Xfinity brand, operates NBCUniversal's television and streaming platforms including Peacock, runs Universal theme parks globally, and provides Sky's direct-to-consumer services in Europe.
